How to figure out any day of the week in your head

  1. Take the last two digits of the year.
  2. Divide by 4, discarding any fraction.
  3. Add the day of the month.
  4. Add the month's key value: JFM AMJ JAS OND 144 025 036 146
  5. Subtract 1 for January or February of a leap year.
  6. Add 0 for 1900's, 6 for 2000's, 4 for 1700's, 2 for 1800's; for other years, add or subtract multiples of 400.
  7. Add the last two digits of the year.
  8. Divide by 7 and take the remainder.

You'll get a number between 0 and 6, which will correspond to one of the weekdays Sunday-Saturday
